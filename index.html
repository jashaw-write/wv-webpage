Copyediting and Proofreading
  Proofreading

  In proofreading your work the focus will be on the following:

   -Correcting typos and mispelled words
   -Replacing improper punctuation

   -Fixing errors in usage - (their, they're, there)

   -Revising miscellaneous details such as formatting, table of contents,
    headings, subheadings and correcting margins etc.
  Copyediting

  In copyediting your work the focus will be on the following:

  -Correcting faulty spelling, grammar and punctuation

  -Addressing areas of text that appear unclear to the reader

  -Suggesting alternative wording to redundancies

  -Ensuring numbers, citations etc., are consistent throughout your writing
